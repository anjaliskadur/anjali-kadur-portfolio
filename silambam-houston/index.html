05 — Community & Craft

Silambam Houston

Silambam Houston is recognized as a Houston Cultural Treasure and Houston's premiere Indian arts organization — promoting classical dance and music, building multicultural audiences, and passing traditions to the next generation. I take on a handful of roles behind the scenes, from keeping the facility running to managing tech, the website, and teaching. Learn more at silambamhouston.org.

Facility Manager

Keep the Cultural Arts Center show-ready — coordinating studio schedules, maintenance, and logistics for classes, rehearsals, Abhyasa performances, and company productions.

Website Manager

Maintain and update silambamhouston.org — programs, events, registration, and the student portal that connect audiences to the Arts Academy and Company.

Lead Tech & Stage Manager

Run technical and stage logistics for performances — from student showcases and Abhyasa recitals to full company productions at venues like the Hobby Center and Miller Outdoor Theater.

Faculty Member

Teach within the Arts Academy, helping students build technique, artistry, and cultural literacy across Silambam's Indian classical dance and music programs.

Digital Treasure Box

Build and curate Silambam's Digital Treasure Hunt — interactive scavenger hunts through Mahabharata characters, mudras, and activity sheets that tie into Storytime With Silambam.

Akka's Alcove

Lead a student mentorship and hangout space where younger artists connect, ask questions, and find guidance from an older peer in the Silambam community.

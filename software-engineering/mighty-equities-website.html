Mighty Equities Website

Mighty Equities is a collaborative family office and evergreen fund that partners with founders across tech, real estate, hospitality, and consumer goods. I designed and built their website end to end — turning a bold, editorial brand into a fast, fully responsive marketing site. The work spanned layout and typography, the image-led hero, page structure, and the site's navigation and calls to action. Live at mightyequities.com.

Homepage & Hero

The landing experience opens on a full-bleed image carousel and the fund's tagline, “Seeking the Relentless,” with a supporting line about partnering with non-conforming overachievers. I built the rotating hero, the layered headline treatment in the brand's serif/italic type, and the primary calls to action — Learn More and Our Portfolio — that route visitors into the rest of the site.

Structure & Navigation

Beyond the homepage, I set up the site's core sections — Philosophy, Portfolio, and Offices — and the navigation that ties them together, alongside the firm's focus areas: capital and financial investment across technology, real estate, hospitality, and consumer goods. The build emphasizes a premium, editorial feel while staying quick to load and readable on phones, tablets, and desktop.
